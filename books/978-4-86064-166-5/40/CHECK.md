## 文法ポイントを身につけたか

- [ ] I met a boy (whose / that) mother is a teacher.
- [ ] The camera that she (has / having) is very nice.
- [ ] I know (that / which) she is from China.
- [ ] This is the car (that was bought / was bought) by him.
